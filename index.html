Healthy India’s 100% pure Liquid 'Egg Whites' are made exclusively from organic eggs. Poultry/Farmers associated with Healthy India Group are fed organic feed to hens. Feed contains no added hormones, antibiotics or steroids, and no animal by-products, recycled or processed foods.

Healthy India’s Liquid Egg Whites are pasteurized, and therefore safe to enjoy uncooked and cooked both. Perfect for whipping, baking, scrambling, omelets, and to consume raw or boil using poacher.

FAQ

FAQ

  • Whole Egg Liquid, Egg White Liquid, Egg Yellow Liquid, Egg Shell Powder.

  • All egg liquid products by Healthy India are pasteurized. That means, they are rapidly heated and held at a minimum required temperature for a specified time. temperature is hot enough to kill bacteria but not enough to cook the eggs. This process does not affect their color, flavor, nutritional value, or use.

  • Yes, they can be consumed uncooked as they are pasteurized but are best in cooked form, especially if served to high risk person like, infants, young children, pregnant women, older adults and person with weakened immune systems.

  • We recommend you check the aroma for freshness prior to each use — refer to the best before date on the bottle/carton and discard if there is any odor present. The aroma of fresh egg whites resembles an eggy or dairy aroma — when egg whites are bad there’s a distinctive odor.

  • Storage: Keep product on the shelves of the fridge and not in the door where the temperature fluctuates a lot.
    Usage: Slight beating is recommended prior to use.
    Handling: Containers must be handled in an appropriate manner to prevent contamination.

  • 3 tbsp of Egg White = 1 whole egg
    2 tbsp of Egg White = 1 egg whites
    ½ cup of egg white = appx 4-5 egg whites

  • Our own personal tests have shown that Egg Whites, if maintained at 40 degrees or lower in the refrigerator, stay good for around 6 months, even when opened. However, we recommend that you use them in a month if opened to assure the best quality product.
